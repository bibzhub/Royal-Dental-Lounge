Our Philosophy

Dentistry with a Conscience.

Royal Dental Lounge was founded on a simple yet profound vision: to eliminate the fear associated with dental visits and provide treatments that are strictly ethical and necessary.

We observed that many patients defer crucial treatments due to dental anxiety or fear of hidden costs. We established this clinic in MLG Square to be a sanctuary of trust. Here, you are not just a patient; you are a guest in our lounge. We take the time to listen, to explain, and to execute treatments with the utmost gentleness.

Chief Dental Surgeon

Dr. Litty Rathnakumar

BDS, Fellowship in Advanced Laser Dentistry

Dr. Litty Rathnakumar is a distinguished name in Kerala's dental landscape. Her career is a unique blend of extensive public service and elite private practice. Having served for years in premier government hospitals, she has successfully managed thousands of complex dental and oral trauma cases, granting her an extraordinary diagnostic depth that few possess.

She later transitioned to advanced private practice, specializing in laser dentistry and painless microscopic endodontics. Known for her incredibly gentle hands and empathetic approach, Dr. Litty is the dentist of choice for patients with severe dental anxiety and children. She believes in treating the person, not just the tooth.
