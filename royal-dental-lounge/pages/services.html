Permanent Restoration

Dental Implants

Dental implants are the gold standard for tooth replacement — titanium roots fused to your jawbone, topped with a natural-looking crown. They look, feel, and function exactly like your own teeth.

Permanent, long-lasting solution (20+ years)
Prevents bone loss after tooth extraction
No impact on adjacent teeth
Natural appearance and bite function
Popular with NRI patients visiting Kerala
