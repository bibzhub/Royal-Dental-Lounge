Frequently Asked Questions

Have doubts? Here's what mostly patients ask us before their visit.

Ask a Question
Does laser dentistry hurt? +
Not at all! Laser dentistry is virtually painless. In many cases, it eliminates the need for drills and anesthesia injections entirely. It’s perfect for patients with dental anxiety.
Do I need an appointment before coming? +
While we do accept walk-in emergencies, we highly recommend booking an appointment. This ensures zero waiting time and allows Dr. Litty to dedicate proper time for your diagnosis.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Cash, all major Credit/Debit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe), and EMI options for larger treatments like Implants and Smile Designing.
Is the clinic easily accessible for wheelchair users? +
Yes. Located at MLG Square, our clinic is easily accessible with no steep stairs entirely on a friendly gradient. We prioritize access for elderly and physically challenged patients.
